About Us

A wonderful serenity has taken possession of my entire soul, like these sweet mornings of spring which I enjoy with my whole heart.

Situated within 4km of the Athurugiriya interchange, Field view hotel allows for a quick much needed urban getaway. With 8 upscale rooms surrounded by tranquility along with a modern, simplistic restaurant and a fully equipped function hall to cater to all the events that you desire. We are more than happy to host any event that you desire along with a team of professionals to help plan and organize every event from start to end.

Luxury | Eco Friendly

Features

King Roooms

8 Modern rooms with own private parking.With a spacious interior with Balconies in the front and rear of the room flood the room with natural light and provides a beautiful view into the paddy fields. The rooms are also fitted with modern amenities such as a Smart Tv, state of the art bathrooms and furniture.

Restaurant

The state of the art restaurant brings you a trip around the world with the flavours. Chefs will cater to all the needs and the tastes of your choice and also you will have the pleasure to dine with nature. The dining area is enclosed with natural vines and trees to give you the feeling as if you are outdoors.

Function Hall

The halls are designed with a keen eye to simplicity and elegance. With the ability to hold any function you desire from weddings to meetings the halls provide the perfect environment for your needs matched along with the chefs and staff to make your function a successful one.

Eco Friendly

With the Modern Waste Management system we recycle and dispose of the waste to reduce the impact on the environment.
Our belief is conservation. As resources such as oil and coal are reducing, we use Solar Energy for our daily tasks.
